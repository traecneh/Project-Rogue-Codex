Rarity System

How item rarity tiers boost base stats and unlock perk eligibility in Project Rogue.

Overview

Project Rogue features seven loot rarities: normal, uncommon, rare, epic, legendary, mythical, and ascendant. Higher tiers roll larger stat bonuses distributed across Strength, Dexterity, and Constitution, and may also qualify for perks.

Each tier owns a signature color in-game. Use the table below to reference their bonus windows and perk chances when tuning drop tables or loot rewards.

Rarity Bonus Stats (Random Split) Perk Eligible?
Normal 0 bonus stats No
Uncommon 2–4 bonus stats No
Rare 5–8 bonus stats No
Epic 9–12 bonus stats Yes — 40% chance
Legendary 13–15 bonus stats Yes — 60% chance
Mythical 16–22 bonus stats Yes — 80% chance
Ascendant 23–30 bonus stats Yes — 100% chance
