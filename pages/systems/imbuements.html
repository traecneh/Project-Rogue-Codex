Imbuements System

Target a specific perk by farming matching Tattered Imbuements, crafting a scroll, and applying it to Epic+ gear.

Targeted Perk Path

Material

Tattered Imbuement

Each tatter is tied to one perk name. Matching tatters are the material used to craft that perk scroll.

Craft

250 Matching Tatters

Spend 250 Matching Tatters in the Craft menu to create one Scroll of Imbuement.

Apply

Epic+ Item

Use the imbuement tool to apply a crafted Scroll of Imbuement to an Epic+ weapon or armor piece.

Recover

25 Tattered Imbuements

Purging or converting a T1 imbuement returns 25 Tattered Imbuements of that perk type.

Imbuement Flow

  1. 1

    Pick a Perk

    Use the Perks page to compare values, stacking rules, and item sources before committing a farm.

  2. 2

    Find Monster Sources

    Monsters with an Uncommon Tatter or Rare Tatter label can drop that perk's tattered material.

  3. 3

    Craft the Scroll

    Use the Craft menu once you have 250 matching tatters for the same perk.

  4. 4

    Apply or Purge

    Apply the Scroll of Imbuement to an Epic+ item, or Purge later if you need to recover materials.

Source Mechanics

Uncommon Tatter

Uncommon tatter sources are the faster path, with roughly a 1 in 10 drop trigger when eligible.

Rare Tatter

Rare tatter sources are slower, with roughly a 1 in 20 drop trigger when eligible.

Level Scaling

On a trigger, quantity scales from 2 up to the higher of 3 or the monster's level divided by 10.

Tier Roll Odds

Crafting targets the perk, not the tier. The Scroll of Imbuement still rolls a tier when applied.

Common Result

80% T1

Most crafted scrolls land as T1, so the system gives reliable perk targeting but not guaranteed high tier.

Strong Result

15% T2

T2 is the realistic upgrade outcome when crafting several scrolls for an important perk.

Best Result

5% T3

T3 is rare enough that saving the best item base for repeated attempts can matter.

Targeting Decisions

Start with Perks

  • Choose the perk effect first, then check whether your gear already supplies it.
  • Remember the three-copy perk cap; race bonuses count toward the cap.
  • Use the Build Planner when deciding whether a crafted imbuement is worth a slot.

Check the Farm

  • Prefer monsters that match your desired perk and have practical kill speed.
  • Higher-level monsters drop larger tatter bundles when a source triggers.
  • Rare Tatter sources may be correct for a build, but they usually need more farming time.
