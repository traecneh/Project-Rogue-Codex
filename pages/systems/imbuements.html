Imbuements System

Guaranteed perk targeting via Tattered Imbuements and crafted scrolls.

Imbuements

Imbuements are new in PR 2.0 (2025). Balance is ongoing; treat the numbers below as guidance.

Perk Focus

Best Path

Perks remain the strongest power path. Only three of any perk type can be active; race bonuses count toward this cap.

Menu

Press [R]

Open the Imbuement interface with R and work through Tool: Imbue, Tool: Craft, and Tool: Purge.

Eligibility

Epic or Higher

An item must be Epic rarity or better to receive an imbuement.

Craft and Apply

Cost

250 Needed

You need 250 of a single Tattered Imbuement type to craft its Scroll of Imbuement in Tool: Craft.

Perk Tiers

Roll Outcome

Scrolls roll perk tiers at 80% T1, 15% T2, and 5% T3 to keep upgrades feeling progressive.

Apply

Tool: Imbue

Use the Imbue tool to attach the crafted scroll to your Epic+ item; Purge removes an applied imbuement if needed.

Where Imbuements Come From

Tagged Monsters

Perk Sources

Only monsters with a perk set in their data can drop Tattered Imbuements. They can be uncommon (1/10), rare (1/20), both, or neither.

Drop Volume

Level Scaling

On a drop, monsters grant between two and the higher of three and their level divided by ten. Higher-level and elite monsters drop more.

Material

Tattered Imbuements

These collectable materials carry a specific perk and are turned into Scrolls of Imbuement via Tool: Craft.

Example

Bloodthirster

Hell Spawn can drop Tattered Imbuement [Bloodthirster]; collect 250 to craft a scroll, then apply it with Tool: Imbue.

Illustrative Drop Math

Numbers shift with balance; use these as rough expectations.

Formula

Uncommon Case

Uncommon Tattered Imbuements drop on roughly 1 out of 10 kills. Each proc yields between two and max(3, monster level / 10) imbuements.

Level 30

Beholder

About 2-3 imbuements on a 1/10 trigger (~0.25 per kill), or around 1,000 kills to reach 250 for a scroll.

Level 90

Hell Spawn

About 2-9 imbuements on a 1/10 trigger (~0.55 per kill), or roughly 450-455 kills to reach 250 for a scroll.

Treat these figures as provisional; live balance may change them.
