Deconstruct System

Convert items into Ascendency Shards in the Deconstruct menu.

How Deconstruct Works

Basics

Shard Conversion

Deconstruct turns gear into Ascendency Shards; higher rarity and level grant more shards.

Press [R], open the Deconstruct tab, and see eligible backpack items with their shard payouts.

Finality

No Takebacks

Every deconstruct is final; shards go straight into your inventory and the original item cannot be recovered.

Dirty Loot

Half Value

Deconstructing dirty looted items returns half the normal shard value.

Imbuements

T1 Returns

Deconstructing T1 Imbuements from the Craft menu returns 25 Tattered Imbuements.

Open & Inspect

Select an item in the Deconstruct list to see its shard payout before confirming.

You can deconstruct individually by clicking Select and then Decon.

Bulk Deconstruct

Use Deconstruct All near the bottom to deconstruct everything in your backpack. It takes three presses of Deconstruct All, then a fourth click to confirm the action.
