Deconstruct System

Convert unwanted gear into Ascendency Shards without losing track of what is worth keeping.

What Deconstruct Returns

Currency

Shard Conversion

Deconstruct turns gear into Ascendency Shards; higher rarity and level grant more shards.

Value

Inspect Payout

Select an eligible backpack item to see its shard payout before confirming.

Dirty Loot

Half Value

Deconstructing dirty looted items returns half the normal shard value.

Imbuements

T1 Returns

Deconstructing T1 Imbuements from the Craft menu returns 25 Tattered Imbuements.

Finality

No Takebacks

Every deconstruct is final; shards go to your inventory and the original item cannot be recovered.

What Affects Value

Rarity & Level

Higher rarity and item level grant more Ascendency Shards.

Dirty State

Dirty looted items return half of the normal shard payout.

Item Data

Weapon and armor detail pages show deconstruction and promotion values for each item.

Imbuement Type

T1 imbuements convert into Tattered Imbuements instead of regular gear shards.

Deconstruct or Keep

Deconstruct

  • The item has poor base value, weak rarity, or no useful perk.
  • The shard payout is more useful than trying to improve the item.
  • You need Ascendency Shards for another item's promotion path.

Keep / Improve

  • The item has a strong source, high max rarity, or a perk you want.
  • The current roll is bad, but the item is worth a Re-Roll attempt.
  • The item can still Ascend and fits a build you are planning.

Deconstruct Flow

  1. 1

    Open the tab

    Press [R], then open the Deconstruct tab.

  2. 2

    Inspect payout

    Select an eligible backpack item to view the shard payout before committing.

  3. 3

    Single item

    Click Select, then Decon to deconstruct one item.

  4. 4

    Bulk Safety

    Deconstruct All requires three presses, then a fourth click to confirm the action.

Bulk Safety

Use Deconstruct All only after checking the backpack. It targets everything eligible in your backpack, and the action cannot be undone after confirmation.
