Active vs Passive

Active State

10 chunks (160 tiles)

  • The encounter is Active while a player is within range of its center.
  • Most encounters increase their spawn rate while Active.
  • Going Active after 4+ minutes Passive triggers a Just Activated spawn pulse.

Passive State

Out of Range

  • If no player is in range, the encounter remains Passive.
  • Leaving range starts the elite grace countdown when the encounter had Prolonged Activity.
  • Re-entering the 10-chunk radius during grace refreshes the timer.

Escalation Flow

  1. 1

    Enter range

    A player moves within 10 chunks, making the encounter Active.

  2. 2

    Hold pressure

    After 1 minute of continuous Active time, the encounter gains Prolonged Activity.

  3. 3

    Unlock variants

    Prolonged Activity allows Elite, Corrupted, and Elite+ monsters to spawn.

  4. 4

    Leave or refresh

    The encounter keeps eligibility through a 6-minute grace window after going Passive.

Variant Rules

Elite

Purple Lightning

  • Spawn chance: 1 in 33
  • 3x health and 1.5x damage
  • 3x drop rate
  • Base Rarity: Uncommon

Corrupted

Green Lightning

  • Spawn chance: 1 in 66
  • Spawn time: Night only
  • 5x health and 1.75x damage
  • 5x drop rate and Corrupts loot
  • Base Rarity: Uncommon

Elite+

Yellow Lightning

  • Spawn chance: 1 in 100
  • 10x health and 2x damage
  • 10x drop rate
  • Base Rarity: Rare

Despawns and Leashes

Inactivity

4-Minute Rule

Monsters despawn after four continuous minutes without moving or attacking.

Uptime

1-Hour Cap

Any monster exceeding one hour of uptime despawns regardless of activity.

Exceptions

Spires

Demon Spires, Dragon Spires, and Master Spires ignore the 4-minute inactivity rule.

Bosses

Locale Leash

Dragging a boss outside its locale forces an instant leash, reset, and full health restore.

Why It Matters

Encounter state controls more than spawn volume. It is also the path into elite variants, corrupted loot, and higher drop-rate windows, so farming routes should account for uptime and range resets.
