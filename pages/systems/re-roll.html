Re-Roll System

Spend reroll shards and stones to spin new stat rolls on gear.

How Re-Roll Works

Re-roll replaces an item's current stat rolls. Bring the required shard currency and a reroll stone, then select your item.

Currency

Reroll Shards

Earned from blacksmith NPCs in towns; required for each reroll attempt.

Stone

Purchase & Carry

Buy a reroll stone from a town shopkeeper and keep it in your inventory.

Menu

Select the Item

Open the Reroll menu and choose the item you want to reroll.

Outcome

Fresh Stat Spread

Stats are rerolled within the item's tier—hope for better spread and totals.
