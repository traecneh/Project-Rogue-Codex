Re-Roll System

Spend reroll shards and stones to replace an item's current stat spread.

What Re-Roll Uses

Currency

Reroll Shards

Earned from blacksmith NPCs in towns; required for each reroll attempt.

Stone

Reroll Stone

Buy a reroll stone from a town shopkeeper and keep it in your inventory.

Menu

Select the Item

Open the Reroll menu and choose the item you want to reroll.

Outcome

Fresh Stat Spread

Stats are rerolled within the item's current rarity tier.

What Changes

Changes

  • Stat Spread is replaced with a fresh roll.
  • Strength, Dexterity, and Constitution can land in a different distribution.
  • The total stat value can move within the current rarity tier's range.

What Does Not Change

  • Item Identity stays the same weapon or armor.
  • Current Rarity is not promoted by re-rolling.
  • Max Rarity, drop source, craft source, and base item values stay tied to the item.

Re-Roll Flow

  1. 1

    Pick a keeper

    Start with an item whose base stats, rarity, source, or perk make it worth improving.

  2. 2

    Bring resources

    Carry the required reroll shards and a reroll stone before opening the menu.

  3. 3

    Replace the roll

    Select the item and commit only when you are ready to replace its current stat spread.

  4. 4

    Check the result

    Compare the new Strength, Dexterity, and Constitution spread against the build you are chasing.

Before You Roll

  • Check the item's current rarity on the item detail page.
  • Use the Rarity page to understand the stat range for that tier.
  • Know which stat your build actually needs before replacing a decent roll.
  • Consider Deconstruct when the item itself is not worth keeping.

When Re-Roll Helps

  • The item is strong, but its bonus stats landed in the wrong places.
  • A build is short on Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution.
  • You want a better spread without changing the item, source, or rarity tier.
