Corruption System

How corruption replaces innate perks, where corrupted items drop, and how to cleanse them.

Corruption Rules

Override

Innate Swap

The corruption system lets an item's innate perk be overridden with a different perk or a higher-tier version.

Designated

Assigned Corruption

Items can define a specific corruption perk; e.g., Dark Sword's innate is Epidemic T3 and its corruption can be Crimson Feast T1.

Cleanse

Purge Tool

Corrupted items can be cleansed back to their innate perk via the Purge tool.

Availability

Drop Sources

Corrupted items currently drop at night and from Corrupted Elites at all times.
