PVP Systems and Restrictions

Project Rogue keeps world PVP lethal but readable with safe zones, decay timers, and criminal rules.

World

Open Combat

Fight anywhere outside towns. Safe zones show a dove icon on the bottom-right HUD.

Death

Respawn + Drops

On death you return to your marked spawn, drop your backpack, and normally drop one equipped item. If a weapon only works while a full armor set is worn, losing any set piece makes you drop that weapon too.

Decay

Body Timers

Bodies become lootable after 4 minutes and fully drop to the ground after 10 minutes.

Loot Windows

4-Minute Loot

Marked Items

Anything taken at 4 minutes is flagged as looted, drops on every death, and needs trading back to cleanse.

10-Minute Drop

Clean Reset

If untouched until 10 minutes, the body falls and items become clean—no loot flag carried forward.

Criminal Death

No Early Loot

When you die criminal, items are not flagged looted at 4 minutes; they can be taken clean, and you respawn at the nearest criminal gate.

Criminal Status

Flag

Going Crim

Attacking any player or NPC turns your name grey. Status ends after 5 minutes out of sight of players/NPCs.

Counts

Offense Tracking

Last-hit kill grants 2 counts; looting a non-criminal item adds 1. No counts for killing/looting temporary criminals.

Jail

50+ Counts

Guards jail you at 50 counts. If your temporary criminal flag has expired they drag you to jail; if you still have an active criminal status they execute you on sight. Wait it out or smash rocks and dump them in the crusher to work counts down.

Tracking & Community

Feed

Live Calls

In Discord > Project Rogue Feeds: global-chat mirrors in-game global chat live; kill-feed posts kills, sprees, and kill/assist ratios for both killer and victim.

Leaderboard

Discord Feeds

leaderboard-levels (top 20), leaderboard-kills (top 40), and leaderboard-shards (top 40) update every 24 hours with ladder-style rankings.
