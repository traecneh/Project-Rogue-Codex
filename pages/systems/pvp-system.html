PVP System

Open-world PVP rules, loot timers, and criminal consequences at a glance.

PVP at a Glance

World

Open Combat

Players can fight anywhere outside protected towns and marked safe areas.

Safe Zone

Safe Zone Dove

Safe zones show a dove icon on the bottom-right HUD so risk state is readable in-game.

Death

Backpack + Gear

Death returns you to your marked spawn, drops your backpack, and normally drops one equipped item.

Decay

Body Timers

Bodies become lootable after 4 minutes and fully drop after 10 minutes.

Safe Zones and Flagging

Protected Areas

Read the HUD

  • Town safety is communicated by the dove icon.
  • Outside safe zones, PVP is open-world and lethal.
  • Safe-zone status matters before duels, escorts, and gear swaps.

Criminal Status

Grey Name Flag

  • Attacking any player or NPC turns your name grey.
  • Status clears after 5 minutes out of sight of players and NPCs.
  • Temporary criminals can be killed or looted without adding offense counts.

Death and Loot Flow

  1. 1

    Death resolves

    You respawn at your marked spawn, or at the nearest criminal gate if you died criminal.

  2. 2

    Gear drops

    Your backpack drops, plus one equipped item in normal death rules.

  3. 3

    Looted Item Flag

    Items taken from the body at 4 minutes are flagged looted and drop on every death.

  4. 4

    Clean Ground Drop

    If untouched until 10 minutes, the body drops items clean with no carried loot flag.

Criminal Consequences

Kills

2 counts

The last hit on a kill grants two offense counts.

Looting

1 count

Looting a non-criminal item adds one offense count.

Guards

50+ Counts

Guards jail you once you reach 50 counts.

Active Crim

Execute on Sight

If your criminal status is still active, guards execute you instead of dragging you to jail.

Sentence

Work Counts Down

Wait it out, or smash rocks and dump them in the crusher to reduce offense counts.

Set Gear

Set Weapon Check

If a weapon only works with a full armor set, losing any set piece makes that weapon drop too.
