Chat System

Channel filters, send-mode scope, and quick input controls in one reference.

Chat at a Glance

Open

Press T

Opens chat for sending messages.

Cycle

Press Tab

Cycles the active view through Local, Global, Guild, and All.

Expand

Hold E

Temporarily expands chat to full screen while the key is held.

Global Send

Safe Zone Only

Global messages can only be sent from safe zones.

Viewing Channels

All

Everything

Shows every channel in one scroll.

Local

Nearby Only

Limits the feed to players in your visible area.

Global

World Channel

Shows server-wide messages sent from safe zones.

Guild

Guildmates

Shows only messages from members of your guild.

Send Mode Preview

Pick a send mode to compare audience, channel, and restrictions.

Viewing filters decide what you see; send modes decide who receives your message.

Current Mode Say

Audience: players within your visible area.

Restriction: posts to Local.

Hotkeys and Input Flow

  1. 1

    Open chat

    Press T to start sending a message.

  2. 2

    Choose visibility

    Press Tab to cycle Local, Global, Guild, or All.

  3. 3

    Read more context

    Press and hold E to make chat full screen while the key is held.

Scope Reference

Say

Visible Area

Posts to Local; only players within your visible area see it.

Whisper

Tile Tight

Posts to Local; only players on your tile or the 8 surrounding tiles see it.

Global

Safe Zone Only

Posts to Global; only server-wide messages sent from safe zones can be sent here.

Guild

Guildmates

Posts to Guild; only members of your guild see it.
