Chat System

Filter views, quick-send hotkeys, and channel visibility rules.

Viewing Chat

Switch the feed to focus on what matters.

All

Everything

Shows every channel in one scroll.

Local

Nearby Only

Limits to messages from players in your visible area.

Global

World Channel

Shows server-wide messages sent from safe zones.

Guild

Team Channel

Only messages from members of your guild.

Sending Messages

Hotkey

Open + Cycle

Press T to open chat; press Tab to cycle Local, Global, Guild, or All.

Fullscreen

Hold to Expand

Press and hold E to temporarily make chat full screen while the key is held.

Say

Local Range

Posts to Local; only players within your visible area see it.

Whisper

Tile Tight

Posts to Local; only players on your tile or the 8 surrounding tiles see it.

Global

Safe Zone Only

Posts to Global; can only be sent while you are in a safe zone.

Guild

Guildmates

Posts to Guild; only members of your guild see it.
