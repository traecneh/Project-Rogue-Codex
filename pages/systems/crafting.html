Crafting System

Hammer-and-anvil armor crafting for Frost and Dragon sets.

Armor Crafting Scope

Tools

Hammer & Anvil

Carry a blacksmith hammer, stand adjacent to an anvil, and choose a material stack.

Frost

Ice Crystals

Ice Crystals craft Frost armor pieces.

Dragon

Dragon Scales

Black, Red, Blue, and Green scales craft Dragon armor in the matching look.

Success

100% Success

Crafting succeeds at 100%; materials are not lost to failed attempts.

Outcome

Random Rarity

The completed item appears after about 4 seconds and rolls a random rarity.

Frost vs Dragon Materials

Frost Armor

  • Uses Ice Crystals only.
  • Every slot consumes the same amount listed in the Material Costs section.
  • There is one Frost visual set.

Dragon Armor

  • Uses Dragon Scales.
  • Scale color determines the Dragon armor look.
  • Black, Red, Blue, and Green scales use the same slot costs.

Crafting Flow

  1. 1

    Equip the tool

    Carry a blacksmith hammer and stand next to an anvil.

  2. 2

    Select materials

    Right-click the hammer, then select an Ice Crystal or Dragon Scale stack.

  3. 3

    Pick the slot

    Choose Helm, Gauntlets, Legs, Shield, or Plate from the piece selector.

  4. 4

    Receive the item

    The crafted armor appears after about 4 seconds with a random rarity roll.
