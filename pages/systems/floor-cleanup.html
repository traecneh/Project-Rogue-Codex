Floor Cleanup System (Creeper)

Understand how long dropped loot can remain on the ground before the Creeper removes it.

Creeper Timing

The Creeper is the automated sweep that removes abandoned loot.

Cycle

Sweep Interval

Cleanup runs every 3 minutes.

Check

Undisturbed Timer

Each ground item is checked for its undisturbed time during the sweep.

Removal

Deletion Threshold

Items untouched for more than 8 minutes when the sweep occurs are deleted.

Lifetime

Worst Case

Item reaches 8 minutes just before a sweep: survives about 8 minutes.

Lifetime

Best Case

Item reaches 8 minutes just after a sweep: lasts to the next pass, up to 11 minutes.

Lifetime

Typical Window

Untouched loot remains on the floor for 8–11 minutes.
