Floor Cleanup System (Creeper)

Understand how long dropped loot can remain on the ground before the Creeper removes it.

Creeper Timing

The Creeper is the automated sweep that removes abandoned loot.

  • Runs a cleanup pass every 3 minutes.
  • Evaluates each ground itemâ€™s undisturbed timer during that pass.
  • Deletes any item that has remained untouched for more than 8 minutes at the time of the sweep.

Because the sweep cadence is fixed, effective item lifetime falls within a narrow window.

  • Worst case: Item hits 8 minutes right before a sweep → survives roughly 8 minutes.
  • Best case: Item hits 8 minutes right after a sweep → lasts until the next pass, up to 11 minutes.
  • In practice, untouched loot exists on the floor for 8–11 minutes.

Example: Resetting the Timer

  • Interacting with an item resets its undisturbed timer to 0.
  • Moving a stack to a different tile grants the full 8–11 minute window again, starting from the new location.
