Floor Cleanup System (Creeper)

A compact reference for how long abandoned loot can stay on the ground before the Creeper removes it.

Floor Cleanup at a Glance

System

Creeper

The Creeper is the automated sweep that removes abandoned loot from the floor.

Cycle

3 minutes

Cleanup runs on a recurring 3 minutes sweep interval.

Threshold

More than 8 minutes

Ground items are removed when their undisturbed time is more than 8 minutes during a sweep.

Lifetime

8-11 minutes

Untouched loot normally remains on the floor for 8-11 minutes, with best-case timing up to 11 minutes.

Creeper Timing Rules

1

Item Drops

A dropped item starts accumulating undisturbed time while it remains on the floor.

2

Sweep Checks

Every 3 minutes, the cleanup pass checks each ground item against its undisturbed time.

3

Creeper Removes

If the item has been untouched for more than 8 minutes when the sweep occurs, it is deleted.

Loot Lifetime Flow

  1. 1

    Loot lands

    The item sits on the floor and begins tracking undisturbed time.

  2. 2

    Sweeps pass

    Each 3 minutes cleanup pass checks whether the item has crossed the removal threshold.

  3. 3

    Threshold is reached

    Once the item is more than 8 minutes old and still undisturbed, the next sweep can remove it.

What Resets the Risk

Pickup

Claim the item

Picking up loot removes it from the floor, so it is no longer exposed to Creeper cleanup.

Fresh Drop

New floor item

Newly dropped loot starts its own undisturbed time and follows the same 8-11 minutes window.

Planning

Do not wait

If loot matters, treat 8 minutes as the practical deadline and the remaining sweep window as buffer.
