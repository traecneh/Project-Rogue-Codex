Craft System

Spend Ethereal Shards to buy power items or craft Scrolls of Imbuement.

Ethereal Shards

Ethereal Shards drop in the world and fund every purchase and craft in this menu.

Currency

Shard Sink

All craft purchases and scroll crafting consume Ethereal Shards.

Access

Craft Menu

Open the Craft menu to spend shards, buy items, or craft imbuement scrolls.

Shard Purchases

Buy these consumables directly with Ethereal Shards.

Augment Orb

Guaranteed Perk

Applies a guaranteed T3 perk to an item at random.

Runeshard

Scroll of Regret

Resets some of your orbs when used.

Berserker Potion

AoE Assault

Triggers attacks around you for one minute.

Scrolls of Imbuement

Craft scrolls and apply them to qualifying gear.

Cost

250 Imbuement

Requires 250 of the chosen imbuement type; click Select when you have them.

Craft

Scroll Creation

Use shards to convert your 250 imbuement into a Scroll of Imbuement.

Apply

Epic+ Only

You can only apply an imbuement to items that are Epic rarity or higher.
