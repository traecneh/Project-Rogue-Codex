Craft System

Spend Ethereal Shards to buy power items or craft Scrolls of Imbuement.

Ethereal Shards

Ethereal Shards drop in the world and fund every purchase and craft in this menu.

Currency

Shard Sink

All craft purchases and scroll crafting consume Ethereal Shards.

Access

Craft Menu

Open the Craft menu to spend shards, buy items, or craft imbuement scrolls.

Shard Purchases

Buy these directly with Ethereal Shards.

Augment Orb

100,000 Shards

Imbues a random T3 perk on an item.

Scroll of Regret

25,000 Shards

Returns 5 of each stat back as orbs.

Race Change Scroll

250,000 Shards

Teleports you to Race Change Island.

Use criteria: you must be in a safe zone, not flagged criminal, and remain stationary for 30+ seconds.

On exit, you are transported to Jeel.

Collector's Pouch

100,000 Shards

Automatically loots gold and imbuements.

Berserker Potion (1)

10,000 Shards

Allows you to attack in all directions.

Berserker Potion (5)

40,000 Shards

Allows you to attack in all directions.

Scrolls of Imbuement

Craft scrolls and apply them to qualifying gear.

Cost

Scroll of Imbuement

Requires 250 Tattered Imbuements; craft to imbue a specific perk on an item.

Convert

Tattered Imbuement

Turn any T1 Imbuement into 25 Tattered Imbuements.

Apply

Epic+ Only

You can only apply an imbuement to items that are Epic rarity or higher.
