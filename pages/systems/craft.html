Craft System

The Ascendancy Craft menu: spend Ethereal Shards, buy utility items, and craft Scrolls of Imbuement.

Craft Menu Role

Currency

Ethereal Shards

Ethereal Shards fund every direct purchase and scroll craft in this menu.

Shop

Shard Purchases

Buy utility and progression items directly with Ethereal Shards.

Imbuements

Targeted Perks

Craft Scrolls of Imbuement from matching Tattered Imbuements.

Travel

Race Change

The Race Change Scroll sends eligible characters to Race Change Island.

Ethereal Shard Purchases

Augment Orb

100,000 Shards

Imbues a random T3 perk on an eligible item.

Scroll of Regret

25,000 Shards

Returns 5 of each stat back as orbs.

Race Change Scroll

250,000 Shards

Teleports you to Race Change Island when the safety requirements are met.

  • Character must be in a safe zone.
  • Character must not be flagged criminal.
  • Character must remain stationary for 30+ seconds.
  • Leaving the island transports the character to Jeel.

Collector's Pouch

100,000 Shards

Automatically loots gold and imbuements.

Berserker Potion

10,000 Shards

Buys 1 potion. Allows attacks in all directions.

Berserker Potion Bundle

40,000 Shards

Buys 5 potions at a bundle price.

Scrolls of Imbuement

Craft Scroll

  • 250 Tattered Imbuements of the same perk type craft one Scroll of Imbuement.
  • The resulting scroll targets that specific perk when applied.
  • Scroll crafting happens from the Craft menu, while applying uses the Imbue tool.

Convert T1 Imbuements

  • Any T1 Imbuement can be converted into 25 Tattered Imbuements.
  • This ties the Craft menu directly into Deconstruct and Purge decisions.

Epic+ Only

  • Items must be Epic rarity or higher to receive an imbuement.
  • Check the Rarity page before spending a scroll on a borderline item.

Craft Menu Flow

  1. 1

    Collect shards or tattereds

    Use Ethereal Shards for shop purchases or matching Tattered Imbuements for scroll crafting.

  2. 2

    Choose the path

    Buy a utility item, craft a targeted imbuement scroll, or prepare a Race Change Scroll.

  3. 3

    Check eligibility

    Race changes require safe conditions; imbuements require an Epic+ item.

  4. 4

    Spend deliberately

    These currencies connect to Ascend, Deconstruct, Purge, and build-planning decisions.

Craft vs Crafting

This page covers the Ascendancy Craft menu. The separate Crafting page covers hammer-and-anvil armor crafting with Ice Crystals and Dragon Scales.
