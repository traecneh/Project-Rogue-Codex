Rarity System

How item rarity tiers affect bonus stats, armor class, perk rolls, and item power.

What Rarity Affects

Bonus Stats

STR / DEX / CON

Each tier has a total bonus stat range that splits across Strength, Dexterity, and Constitution.

Bonus AC

Epic+

Armor class bonuses begin at Epic and scale through Ascendant.

Perk Eligibility

Epic+

Epic, Legendary, Mythical, and Ascendant can roll perks at increasing chances.

Max Rarity

Item Bound

Weapons and armors expose their max rarity, which caps upgrade previews and planner choices.

Item Power

Value Scaling

Damage, armor, resistance, and requirement ranges display tier multipliers on item detail pages.
