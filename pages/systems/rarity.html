Rarity System

How item rarity tiers boost base stats and unlock perk eligibility in Project Rogue.

Overview

Project Rogue features seven loot rarities: normal, uncommon, rare, epic, legendary, mythical, and ascendant. Higher tiers roll larger stat bonuses distributed across Strength, Dexterity, and Constitution, and may also qualify for perks.

Each tier carries a signature color in-game. Browse the ladder of rarity cards below to see bonus windows and perk eligibility at a glance.
