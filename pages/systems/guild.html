Guild System

Why guild membership matters in Project Rogue, from PvP protections to coordinated comms.

Core Benefits

As soon as you tag up, the game treats your guildmates as trusted allies. The default PvP rules relax, allowing you to operate as a unit even in chaotic encounters. These perks stay active anywhere in the world.

  • Safe Looting: Looting your guildmate’s body never flags you criminal. You can help them recover or secure key drops without the legal fallout.
  • Accelerated De-crim: You can remove criminal status faster while on-screen with guildmates. Staying near the crew turns the timer in your favor.
  • Target Protection Toggle: Enable guild target protection to ignore allies with friendly fire abilities. It’s perfect for tight skirmishes where misclicks would otherwise wipe a teammate.

Guild Party System

Project Rogue folds party features directly into every guild. You don’t have to juggle separate invites— the moment you join, the interface tracks everyone online and gives you combat clarity.

  • Party Interface: The guild panel shows each member’s name, current health, and max health. A matching overlay hits the minimap and world map with diamond icons so you never lose sight of positioning.
  • Health-based Map Icons: The diamonds adopt a traffic-light color scheme: green above 70% health, yellow between 40%–70%, and red under 40%. Color shifts give instant reads on who needs help.
  • Drop Callouts: Chat pings any loot that rolls with rarity while a guildmate is nearby, so everyone can plan upgrades or trades.
  • Flexible Sub-groups: Break into three sub-teams labeled [A], [B], and [C] directly inside the guild panel. Use them to assign roles or split duty mid-run.
