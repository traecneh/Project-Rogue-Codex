Guild System

Open the guild panel with G and manage your clan at a glance.

Guild Panel Overview

Press G in-game to open the guild panel and view these details.

Identity

Clan Name & Tag

Shows the clan name and tag; the tag appears in front of your character name in-game.

Roster

Member Count

Displays current members as ## / 50 maximum.

Rank

Your Role

Shows your rank: Member, Elder, Council, or Leader.

Leadership

Leader Name

Displays the current leader of the clan.

Roster List

Scroll List

Member Rows

Scrollable list shows each member as [Rank] Character Name.

Guild Actions

Controls sit beneath the roster for quick management.

Invite

Add Member

Add a player to the guild.

Remove

Kick Member

Remove a selected member.

Rank Up

Promote

Raise a member's rank.

Rank Down

Demote

Lower a member's rank.

Party

Guild Party A/B/C

Guild Party button cycles through parties A, B, and C.

Exit

Leave

Leave the guild.
