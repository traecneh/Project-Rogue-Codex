Guild at a Glance

Hotkey

Press G

Opens the guild panel so you can inspect identity, roster, rank, and management controls.

Roster Cap

50 members

The panel shows the active member count as ## / 50.

Ranks

Member to Leader

Guild roles display as Member, Elder, Council, or Leader.

Parties

Party A/B/C

The Guild Party control cycles selected members through Party A, Party B, and Party C.

Identity and Roster

Clan Identity

Name and Tag

The clan name and tag identify the guild. The tag appears in front of your character name in-game.

Roster Rows

[Rank] Character Name

Each roster line shows the member rank beside the character name in a scrollable list.

Leader

Leader Name

The panel lists the current leader separately so ownership is easy to confirm.

Capacity

## / 50

Use the member count to understand whether the guild has room for another invite.

Rank and Management Flow

  1. 1

    Open the panel

    Press G to view clan identity, roster, rank, and leader details.

  2. 2

    Select a member

    Roster actions work from the selected member row when a target is required.

  3. 3

    Adjust standing

    Promote and Demote move members through the displayed rank ladder.

  4. 4

    Assign parties

    Guild Party cycles selected members through Party A, Party B, and Party C.

Operations Reference

Invite

Add Member

Add a player to the guild when the roster has open capacity.

Remove

Kick Member

Remove the selected member from the guild roster.

Rank Up

Promote

Raise the selected member's rank.

Rank Down

Demote

Lower the selected member's rank.

Party

Guild Party

Cycles selected members through Party A, Party B, and Party C.

Exit

Leave

Leave the current guild.
