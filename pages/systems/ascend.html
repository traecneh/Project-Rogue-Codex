Ascend System

Spend Ascendency Shards to raise gear one rarity tier.

How Ascend Works

Ascend raises an item to the next rarity tier when you spend the required Ascendency Shards.

Every item has a maximum rarity tier; identify an item to see whether it can still ascend.

The Ascend menu lists backpack items with shard costs, stat totals, and current rolls. Select items and confirm to move them up a tier.
