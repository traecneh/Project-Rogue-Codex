Races

Stat bonuses, weapon proficiencies, and innate perks per race.

Tier Progression

All racial perks begin at Tier 1 and climb automatically as you level up. You stay in T1 from level 1–70, reach Tier 2 at levels 71–99, and finish at Tier 3 from level 100–105. The perk descriptions below mirror the main perk reference so you get the exact tier scaling in one place.
