Races

Stat packages, weapon affinities, and innate perks that define each playable lineage.

Tier Progression

All racial perks begin at Tier 1 and climb automatically as you level up. You stay in T1 from level 1–69, reach Tier 2 at levels 70–99, and finish at Tier 3 from level 100–105. The perk descriptions below mirror the main perk reference so you get the exact tier scaling in one place.

Racial Bonuses

Human

Adaptable frontliners that balance physical power and survivability, making them reliable in every group composition.

  • Attributes: +5 Strength and +5 Constitution.
  • Weapon Proficiency: +5 to swords and small blades.
  • Perk — Desperation: Deals up to [6/8/10]% bonus damage, scaling with how low your health percentage is.

Tundrian

Northern juggernauts who lean into raw power and thrive in unforgiving climates.

  • Attributes: +10 Strength.
  • Weapon Proficiency: +5 to axes.
  • Perk — Frozen Heart: Increases element-specific resistance by [8/10/12]%.

Brimlock

Veteran defenders whose sturdy frames and blunt mastery make them ideal anchors on the frontline.

  • Attributes: +5 Constitution.
  • Weapon Proficiency: +10 to blunts.
  • Perk — Demon Blood: Increases element-specific resistance by [8/10/12]%.

Komodan

Draconic wardens whose resilient scales and polearm talent make them dependable battle mages or tanks.

  • Attributes: +5 Constitution.
  • Weapon Proficiency: +10 to poles.
  • Perk — Magic Shield: Increases all resistances by [2/3/4]%.

Elf

Agile strikers who convert high Dexterity and pole expertise into relentless offense and crowd control.

  • Attributes: +10 Dexterity.
  • Weapon Proficiency: +5 to poles.
  • Perk — Parry: (6/7/9/10)% chance (based on weapon speed) to parry, reducing damage taken by [70/80/90]%, multiplicative.

Orc

Battle-hardened enforcers with iron constitutions that let them shrug off punishment and keep swinging.

  • Attributes: +10 Constitution.
  • Weapon Proficiency: +5 to blunts.
  • Perk — Hazmat: Increases element-specific resistance by [8/10/12]%.

Gnoll

Tireless skirmishers who mix balanced Constitution with sword skill for sustained frontline pressure.

  • Attributes: +5 Constitution.
  • Weapon Proficiency: +5 to swords.
  • Perk — Rejuvenation: Heals an additional [10/15/20] per Constitution tick, plus [2/3/4] per tick for every 25 player levels; additive.

Dark Elf

Precision duelists who pair deft blade work with potent alchemical support.

  • Attributes: +5 Dexterity.
  • Weapon Proficiency: +5 to small blades.
  • Perk — Alchemist: Potions heal an additional [30/40/50]%, additive.
