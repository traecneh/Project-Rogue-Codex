Races

Playable race bonuses, racial innate perks, and how those bonuses affect checks.

Races at a Glance

Playable Races

8

Each race combines attribute bonuses, weapon proficiency, and one innate perk.

Innate Perks

Tier 1-Tier 3

Racial perks start at Tier 1, reach Tier 2 at level 71, and reach Tier 3 at level 100.

Bonus Range

+5 or +10

Race bonuses can raise displayed attributes or weapon skills beyond trained values.

Requirement Rule

Base values pass equipment checks

Race bonuses are visible on the character, but equipment requirements check base values.
