Constitution Stat

Overview

Constitution is the tank stat. Every point makes you harder to kill by inflating your maximum health pool and accelerating your natural regeneration ticks.

  • Max health uses 20 + (Level * 15) + (Con * 10) + (Str * 2).
  • Passive regeneration powers up by Con / 3 HP every 2 seconds.

Health Scaling
