Bleed Threshold

Trigger

100 Base Strength

Attacks can inflict bleed once base Strength reaches 100.

Chance

Strength / 10%

A character with 150 base Strength has a 15.0% bleed chance per hit.

Damage

7.5% of hit damage

Each bleed stack uses the damage that applied the stack.
