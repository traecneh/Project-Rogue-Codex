Strength Stat

Overview

Strength is the backbone of melee combat and survivability. It raises the raw damage of every swing, increases how much kit you can haul, and even feeds directly into max health.

  • Scales melee damage through the shared multiplier: 1 + (Skill/50) + (Str/100) + (Dex/200).
  • Increases maximum carry weight using 150 + (3 * Strength).
  • Feeds max health together with level and Constitution: 20 + (Level * 15) + (Con * 10) + (Str * 2).

Bleed Threshold

Reaching 100 base Strength unlocks the ability to inflict bleeds with any compatible attack.

  • Trigger: Base Strength >= 100.
  • Chance: (Strength / 10)% per hit, before perks or weapon bonuses.
  • Bleed Damage: 7.5% of the damage that applied the stack.
