Intelligence Stat

Spell damage, resource efficiency, and utility scaling for casters.

Tuned for Season 3 balance build

Overview

Intelligence boosts the potency of arcane, frost, and lightning abilities while expanding the mana pool required to sling them. Crowd-control duration, summon power, and gadget effectiveness also tie into INT scaling.

  • +1 INT = +2 spell power and +4 mana.
  • Control effects last 1% longer for every 8 INT (additive with talents).
  • Summoned allies inherit 0.3% of your INT score as bonus damage.

Magic Schools

Arcane

Burst damage

High coefficients make Arcane Barrage scale the best with INT. Pair with cooldown reduction gear.

Frost

Control

Gains chill duration with INT. Reaching 38 INT ensures perma-slow windows on elites.

Storm

Utility

Lightning skills convert every 10 INT into +3% shock chance, ideal for support casters.

Breakpoints

  • 22 INT: Unlocks second mana bar segment and advanced runes.
  • 30 INT: Soft cap for mana regen; further points go into raw power.
  • 38 INT: Enables Frost Nova perma-freeze combo with supportive traits.
  • 46 INT: Requirement for Cataclysm channel and elder focus gear.

Build Concepts

Arcanist

40 INT / 20 DEX / 15 CON

Glass cannon that uses blink mobility plus arcane volleys for huge burst windows.

Stormcaller

36 INT / 24 CON / 18 STR

Battle-mage hybrid wielding a shock staff; adds self-buffs for survivability.

Frostbinder

34 INT / 22 CON / 20 DEX

Control-focused caster who chains freezes and supports the frontline with slows.
