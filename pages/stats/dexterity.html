Dexterity Stat

Overview

Dexterity is the finesse core for Project Rogue. It feeds the global melee multiplier, fuels crit chance, and bakes in survival for light builds that refuse to wear heavy plate.

  • Increases melee damage through 1 + (Skill/50) + (Str/100) + (Dex/200).
  • Raises crit chance by Dex / 3% for 1.35× damage spikes.
  • Grants passive damage reduction based solely on base Dexterity.

Core Mechanics

Damage

Melee Multiplier

Dexterity adds the Dex/200 slice to the shared multiplier. Pair it with Skill and Strength to keep daggers, bows, and claws scaling into the endgame.

Criticals

Dexterity Crit Chance

Base DEX delivers Dex / 3% chance to crit at 1.35× damage before gear. The more DEX you stack, the more those crit spikes fuel burst rotations.

Mitigation

Innate DR

Even without armor, Dexterity adds a smoothing damage reduction layer that stacks with plate and buffs, letting glass cannons shrug off chip damage.
