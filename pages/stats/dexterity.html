Dexterity Stat

Dexterity contributes to melee damage, crit chance (Base Dex), and damage reduction (Total Dex).

Overview

Key Dexterity effects and formulas.

Damage

Melee Multiplier

Shared multiplier: 1 + (Skill/50) + (Str/100) + (Dex/200).

Critical

Crit Chance

Base Dex / 2.5% crit chance at 1.35x damage.

Mitigation

Damage Reduction

Damage reduction (Total Dex): (Dex * 0.00125) / (1 + Dex * 0.00125).
