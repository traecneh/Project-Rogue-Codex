Dexterity at a Glance

Damage

Melee Multiplier

Shared multiplier: 1 + (Skill / 50) + (Str / 100) + (Dex / 200).

Critical

Crit Chance

Base Dex / 2.5% crit chance at 1.35x damage.

Mitigation

Damage Reduction

(Total Dex * 0.00125) / (1 + Total Dex * 0.00125).
