Project Rogue Timeline

Project Rogue grows from the Dransik/Ashen Empires lineage of 2D sandbox PvP. Filter the timeline when you only want the pre-history, community forks, or Project Rogue era.

Timeline Filter 10 shown
1998 – 1999

Dransik Classic

Ultima-style 2D MMO launches and sparks the community that chases every successor.

2002 – 2003

Ashen Empires Era

Upgraded Dransik enters open beta in September 2002 and rebrands as Ashen Empires with pay servers in March 2003.

2004 – 2006

Classic Goes Dark & Returns

Dransik Classic shuts down, then resurfaces in April 2006 under Iron Will / Pixel Mine.

2005

Lothgar Online v1

Jason Ely leaves the Dransik team and ships Lothgar Online as a spiritual clone.

Mid-2000s

Vorlia Online

Fans spin up Vorlia Online, a grassroots Dransik-like MMO that later becomes the foundation for Project Rogue.

2013

Elderlands Alpha

Ely's new 2D engine enters open alpha, fusing retro combat with modern tech.

2016

Lothgar Reboot Plans

While leading Asylumsoft on Elderlands, Ely announces a return of Lothgar Online styled after classic Dransik.

Mar 20 2017

Lothgar Relaunch Falters

Relaunch arrives with hype but fizzles; players cite direction issues and look elsewhere.

Jul 2017

Project Rogue Begins

Arakard and Acry reboot Vorlia as Project Rogue: 100-level cap, revamped stat math, Dransik-era systems, and a PvP logout timer.

2017 – Today

Fresh Wipes & Live Upkeep

Regular wipes, balance passes, and content drops keep the server active; each fresh start echoes the early days.
